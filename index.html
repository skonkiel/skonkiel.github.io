Biography

Stacy Konkiel is a Senior Data Analyst at Altmetric. Stacy is experienced in translating organizational values and goals to measurable outcomes, presenting to and collaborating with global teams, and analyzing, visualizing, and explaining complex data to help others understand organizational impact. Stacy co-founded the HuMetricsHSS initiative and Metrics Toolkit. Previously, Stacy worked with teams at Our Research (Impactstory), Indiana University & PLOS.
